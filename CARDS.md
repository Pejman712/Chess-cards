# Chess Power-Up Cards

Ether is the in-game currency: you earn **+1 per square moved**, the **piece's value on capture**, and **+5 for giving check**.

## Piece Powers (left panel)

Each can be used once per game — drag it onto one of your pieces. After use, click the card to refill it for its cost.

| Card | Cost | Power |
|---|---|---|
| **Pawntastic** | 1 | A pawn charges 1–4 squares forward in a single move. |
| **Windknight** | 3 | A knight moves twice in one turn. |
| **Bishock** | 3 | A bishop zaps every piece along its diagonals (kings survive). You collect the destroyed pieces' value. |
| **Rookdemon** | 5 | A rook leaves a burning trail on its next 2 moves. Fire kills any non-king piece standing in it. |
| **Queentum** | 9 | The queen teleports to any legal square on the board. |
| **LongLiveTheKing** | 12 | The king escapes to a random empty corner and spawns pawns on every empty square around it. Works even when checkmated! |

## Power Cards (right panel)

Bought with Ether and played directly — click instant cards, drag targeted ones onto the board.

| Card | Cost | Power |
|---|---|---|
| **Gambit** | 10 | Sacrifice as many of your own pieces as you like, one click at a time, for double their value each. |
| **Chrisma** | 15 | Enemy pieces adjacent to your king switch to your side, and you pocket their value. |
| **ExtraBlood** | 15 | Passive: all your capture Ether is doubled for the rest of the game. |
| **Hot Potato** | 15 | A random enemy piece (never the king) is armed; it explodes in 2 turns. |
| **I Feel Safe** | 15 | Passive: at the end of each of your turns, gain 3 Ether per friendly piece next to your king. |
| **Insurance** | 15 | Passive: the first piece you lose each turn pays you double its value in Ether. |
| **Nope** | 15 | Cancels the opponent's last card. If you're in check instead, it destroys the checking piece. |
| **Pandora's Hand** | 15 | Discard your whole hand, draw that many random cards, and play one of them for free. |
| **AbsoluteProtection** | 20 | Your pieces cannot be captured for one opponent turn — any attacker dies instead. |
| **Communism** | 20 | Both players' Ether is pooled and split evenly (odd Ether rounds to you). |
| **Mirror Match** | 20 | Copy the last instant card the opponent played — for free. |
| **Solo** | 20 | If only your king remains, you win on the spot. |
| **Switchero** | 20 | Swaps ownership of every piece on the board. |
| **TheDramatic** | 20 | Mark one of your pieces. If it is captured, the capturer dies with it. |
| **TimeTraveler** | 20 | Rewinds the board 3 turns into the past. |
| **Hydra** | 25 | Passive: for the rest of this turn, losing one of your pieces spawns two pawns next to where it fell. |
| **Possession** | 25 | Take full control of the opponent's very next turn. |
| **Armageddon** | 30 | A meteor flattens a 3×3 area (kings survive), leaves fire behind, and pays you the destroyed pieces' value. |
| **Earthquake** | 30 | Every piece on the board slides one rank toward its own back row in a chain reaction; whatever's blocking the way is crushed (kings block but never die). |
| **Gravity Storm** | 30 | Pick a point: every piece is pulled toward it and stops at a piece or wall, forming a ring around the point. The point stays empty (any piece on it is crushed) and cannot be a king. |
| **InZone** | 30 | One piece chains capture after capture in a single turn — but dies when the streak ends. |
| **Propaganda** | 30 | Passive: at the start of each of your turns, a 50% chance to convert one enemy piece to your side. |
| **Shuffle Up** | 35 | Every non-king piece on the board is picked up and redealt at random among those same squares. |
| **I Guess** | 40 | Permanently take 2 extra moves every turn. |
| **The Prophecy** | 40 | Every one of your pawns is promoted to a queen. |
| **Plague** | 60 | At the start of each of your turns, one random enemy piece (never the king) dies. |
| **Capitalism** | 100 | Money wins: pay 100 Ether and take the game instantly. |
